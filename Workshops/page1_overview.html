Core AI Fluency Certificate

Customizing AI for Consistency & Personalization

Module Overview

This module focuses on shaping AI interactions for predictable control and consistent behavior, using ChatGPT as the primary example tool. Participants learn how to design prompts, persistent settings, and lightweight custom agents that align with their academic, administrative, or creative goals—without outsourcing their professional judgment.

How This Fits in the Certificate

  • Module 1 builds a realistic mental model of how generative AI works.
  • Module 2 treats AI as a collaborator for exploratory, iterative work.
  • Module 3 (this module) emphasizes consistency, control, and workflows.
  • Module 4 extends these skills into multimodal AI tools and media.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Differentiate prompt-level, persistent, and structural customization strategies.
  • Design prompt patterns and templates that enforce consistent tone, structure, and quality.
  • Implement persistent personalization settings in ChatGPT responsibly and transparently.
  • Decide when a custom GPT, uploaded dataset, or simple template is the right tool for the job.
  • Identify and mitigate privacy, bias, and overconfidence risks introduced by customization.
  • Build one reusable workflow artifact (prompt library, instruction set, or custom GPT concept).

Estimated Time & Format

  • Estimated time: 60–90 minutes
  • Format: Short mini-lectures, guided hands-on lab, scenario analysis, and reflection
