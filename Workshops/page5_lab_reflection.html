Workflow Lab & Reflection

Choose One Workflow to Design

Select one option below and sketch a concrete AI-assisted workflow.

Option A: Prompt Library

  1. List 3–5 recurring tasks in your teaching or research.
  2. Write a structured prompt pattern for each task.
  3. Test the patterns and refine them based on the outputs.
  4. Store them somewhere you can easily reuse and share.

Option B: Persistent Instruction Set

  1. Draft custom instructions that describe how ChatGPT should support your work.
  2. Emphasize tone, structure, and the kinds of questions it should ask you.
  3. Run 2–3 test conversations to see how the behavior changes.
  4. Revise your instructions to reduce friction and risk.

Option C: Custom GPT Concept

  1. Identify a repeating, well-defined task (e.g., syllabus review, discussion prompts).
  2. Specify:
    • What inputs it takes
    • What outputs it should produce
    • Any datasets or documents it needs
  3. Draft the system instructions you would provide to your GPT.
  4. List the risks and how you would communicate them to users.

Reflection

  • What did you decide not to automate or customize, and why?
  • How will you explain your use of AI to students or colleagues?
  • What is one small, low-risk experiment you will try in the next month?
