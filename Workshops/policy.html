Syllabus Policy Templates

The Provost's Office advises faculty to explicitly state AI expectations. Choose the tier that best fits your learning objectives.

Tier 1: Prohibited

Academic Integrity Focus

Best for courses where foundational skills (writing, math, coding) must be developed without assistance.

"All work submitted in this course must be your own. The use of generative AI tools is strictly prohibited for all assignments. Any use of AI will be treated as a violation of the UIUC Student Code."
Tier 2: Managed Use

Collaborative & Transparent

Best for research-heavy courses where AI can assist with brainstorming or grammar check but not drafting.

"AI tools may be used for brainstorming or refining research questions. However, final submissions must be your original writing. Students must include a disclosure statement detailing any AI assistance."

Illinois Disclosure Framework

Per UIUC Best Practices, when AI is permitted, students should provide the following at the end of their assignment:

  • Tool Name: (e.g., Gemini 1.5, ChatGPT-4)
  • Method of Use: (e.g., "Refining my outline")
  • Verification: "I have manually verified all citations and facts for accuracy."
