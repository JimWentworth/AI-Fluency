Temperature

Control the creativity and randomness of AI responses

Temperature is a parameter (usually between 0 and 1) that controls how deterministic or random an AI model's outputs are.

Temperature Scale

0.0
Deterministic

0.3

1.0

Very Creative

How It Works

Low Temperature (0.0 - 0.3)

Behavior: Always picks the most likely next word. Responses are consistent and focused.

Best for: Factual answers, code generation, data extraction, classification tasks

Medium Temperature (0.4 - 0.7)

Behavior: Balances between likely choices and variety.

Best for: General conversation, explanations, summaries

High Temperature (0.8 - 1.0)

Behavior: Considers less-likely word choices. More surprising outputs.

Best for: Creative writing, brainstorming, poetry
