NotebookLM & Illinois Chat for Academic Success

Google NotebookLM

AI-powered note-taking and research tool that helps you understand and work with your documents.

Key Features

  • Upload PDFs, Google Docs, websites, YouTube transcripts
  • Source-grounded answers with citations
  • Automatic summaries and study guides
  • AI-generated podcast-style audio overviews
  • Private by default - Google doesn't use your data for training

Perfect For:

  • Exam Prep: Upload course materials and ask practice questions
  • Research Papers: Add sources and synthesize information
  • Literature Review: Compare themes across multiple papers
  • Lecture Catch-up: Upload slides and audio to create notes

Illinois Chat

Your campus-specific AI tool combining university knowledge with AI capabilities.

Key Features

  • Campus knowledge - policies, procedures, resources
  • Academic support - writing, research, problem-solving
  • NetID authentication for secure access
  • 24/7 availability
Academic Integrity Reminder: Use AI to learn and understand, not to bypass learning. Always follow your course's AI usage policies.
