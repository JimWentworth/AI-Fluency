Current major decisions

  • Module arc: Understanding → Collaborating → Designing → Expanding
    • Rationale: increases participant responsibility and transfer across contexts.
  • Module 3 framing: “Designing Consistent AI Interactions” (not “Customizing tools”)
    • Rationale: emphasizes interaction design, reuse, accountability, and documentation—beyond cosmetic or tool-specific tweaks.
