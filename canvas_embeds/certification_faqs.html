AI Fluency Certificate – Frequently Asked Questions

If your question is not answered below please reach out to us at citl-info@illinois.edu.

CITL's AI Fluency Certifcate is a flexible program that teaches foundational AI concepts, effective collaboration strategies, customization techniques, and multimodal skills. Learners complete four core modules plus two electives, and a learning portfolio by combining asynchronous content with optional live workshops.

The certificate is open to UIUC students, faculty, and staff with a NetID. No prior AI or technical background is required.

Most participants finish in 15–18 hours total, depending on elective choices and workshop participation. The four core modules offer roughly 90-minutes worth of content and activities.

You must complete all four core modules:

  • Understanding What GenAI Is — and Isn’t
  • Collaborative Iteration with GenAI
  • Customizing AI for Consistency
  • Engaging with Multimodal GenAI

You choose two electives aligned with your interests. Each elective requires approximately 60 minutes of engagement and a short reflection or artifact. Electives may include workshops, campus AI events, discipline-specific sessions, or external certificate programs like Google, IBM and LinkedIn Learning.

No traditional grades are assigned. You complete small activities, short quizzes or reflections to demonstrate understanding.
